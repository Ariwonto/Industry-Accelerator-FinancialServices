Microsoft Dynamics 365 Banking Accelerator

Overview

  • Banking model: banking entities combine with the Microsoft Common Data Model to provide a consistent, standardized unified view of data across sources.
  • Samples: apps, dashboards, and other examples are included for you to use to jumpstart your app development. Samples are provided for Commercial and Retail Banking.
  • Connected experiences: experiences across Dynamics 365, Azure, AI, Power BI, PowerApps, and Office 365 Proposal Manager for you to see what is possible.
  • Open Banking: interoperable with several service domains of BIAN to help unlock new Open Banking opportunities.
